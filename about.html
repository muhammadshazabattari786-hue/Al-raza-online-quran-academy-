About Al Raza Online Quran Academy

Al Raza Online Quran Academy is a professional and global level online institution that provides Quranic education and Islamic training to Muslims worldwide from the comfort of their homes.

Our mission is to deliver authentic Quran recitation with Tajweed, Hifz, and Islamic education to people of all ages using modern digital platforms like Skype, Zoom, and WhatsApp.

Our Vision & Mission

🌟 Spiritual Growth

Our goal is to develop true love for Allah Almighty and His beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in the hearts of students.

📚 Perfect Tajweed

Teaching every student Quran with proper Makharij and Tajweed rules is our top priority.

✨ Islamic Character

We don't just teach Quran, we also instill Islamic character, prayer habits, and good manners in children and adults.
