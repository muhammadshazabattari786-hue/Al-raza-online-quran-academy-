40 Essential Duas Every Muslim Should Know
Daily Duas with Arabic, Transliteration & Meaning - Connect with Allah All Day
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 16 Min Read 📚 Daily Duas ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Dua is the Weapon of a Believer

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "Dua is the essence of worship." Yet many Muslims only know 3-4 duas: Bismillah, Alhamdulillah, and dua after food. But Islam gave us specific duas for every moment – waking up, sleeping, entering bathroom, starting journey, feeling sad, visiting sick.

When you know these duas, your entire day becomes worship. Eating becomes ibadah, sleeping becomes ibadah, even going to bathroom becomes ibadah. This article gives you 40 most essential duas every Muslim should memorize with Arabic, transliteration, meaning, and when to recite them. Start with 5 duas this week, add 5 more next week.

وَقَالَ رَبُّكُمُ ادْعُونِي أَسْتَجِبْ لَكُمْ
"And your Lord says, 'Call upon Me; I will respond to you.'"
Surah Ghafir, Ayah 60

Allah promises to answer. But we must call Him with words Prophet taught us. Let's begin with daily routine duas.

1

Dua When Waking Up

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ
Alhamdulillahil-ladhi ahyana ba'da ma amatana wa ilayhin-nushoor
All praise is for Allah who gave us life after death and to Him is the resurrection.
⏰ When: Immediately after opening eyes in morning | 📌 Benefit: Thank Allah for new day, remember Akhirah
2

Dua Before Sleeping

بِاسْمِكَ رَبِّي وَضَعْتُ جَنْبِي وَبِكَ أَرْفَعُهُ
Bismika rabbi wada'tu janbi wa bika arfa'uh
In Your name my Lord, I lie down and in Your name I rise.
⏰ When: Before sleeping | 📌 Benefit: Protection through night, angels guard you
3

Dua Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
Bismillah
In the name of Allah.
⏰ When: Before first bite | 📌 Benefit: Allah puts Barakah in food, Shaytan cannot eat with you
4

Dua After Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنِي هَذَا وَرَزَقَنِيهِ مِنْ غَيْرِ حَوْلٍ مِّنِّي وَلَا قُوَّةٍ
Alhamdulillahil-ladhi at'amani hadha wa razaqanihi min ghayri hawlin minni wa la quwwah
All praise is for Allah who fed me this and provided it without any might or power from me.
⏰ When: After finishing meal | 📌 Benefit: Sins forgiven, gratitude to Allah
5

Dua Entering Bathroom

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ، اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ
Bismillah, Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-khubuthi wal-khaba'ith
In the name of Allah. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from male and female devils.
⏰ When: Before entering toilet | 📌 Benefit: Protection from Shayateen in washroom
6

Dua Leaving Bathroom

غُفْرَانَكَ
Ghufranak
I seek Your forgiveness.
⏰ When: After leaving toilet | 📌 Benefit: Thank Allah for ability to relieve, seek forgiveness for time spent
7

Dua When Leaving Home

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ
Bismillahi tawakkaltu alallah wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah
In the name of Allah, I put my trust in Allah, and there is no power nor strength except with Allah.
⏰ When: Before stepping out of door | 📌 Benefit: Angels protect you, Shaytan cannot harm you whole day
8

Dua Entering Masjid

اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ
Allahumma iftah li abwaba rahmatik
O Allah, open for me the doors of Your mercy.
⏰ When: Entering mosque with right foot first | 📌 Benefit: Allah's mercy surrounds you in prayer
9

Dua Leaving Masjid

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ
Allahumma inni as'aluka min fadlik
O Allah, I ask You from Your bounty.
⏰ When: Leaving mosque with left foot first | 📌 Benefit: Seek Allah's blessings after worship
10

Dua When Angry

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ
A'udhu billahi minash-shaytanir-rajeem
I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Shaytan.
⏰ When: Feeling anger rising | 📌 Benefit: Anger is from Shaytan, this extinguishes it
11

Dua for Parents

رَّبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا
Rabbir-ham huma kama rabbayani sagheera
My Lord, have mercy upon them as they raised me when I was small.
⏰ When: After every prayer, thinking of parents | 📌 Benefit: Rewards reach parents in grave
12

Dua When Sick

أَسْأَلُ اللَّهَ الْعَظِيمَ رَبَّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ أَنْ يَشْفِيَكَ
As'alullahal-'azeem rabbal-'arshil-'azeem an yashfeek
I ask Allah the Mighty, Lord of the Mighty Throne, to cure you.
⏰ When: Visiting sick person, recite 7 times | 📌 Benefit: If death not decreed, Allah cures
13

Dua for Guidance - Istikhara Short

اللَّهُمَّ خِرْ لِي وَاخْتَرْ لِي
Allahumma khir li wakhtar li
O Allah, choose what is best for me and decree it for me.
⏰ When: Confused about decision | 📌 Benefit: Allah chooses best for you
14

Dua When Starting Journey

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ
Subhanalladhi sakhkhara lana hadha wa ma kunna lahu muqrineen
Glory be to Him who has subjected this to us, and we could never have it by our own efforts.
⏰ When: Boarding car, plane, train | 📌 Benefit: Safe journey, protection from accident
15

Dua for Knowledge

رَّبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا
Rabbi zidni ilma
My Lord, increase me in knowledge.
⏰ When: Before studying, entering class | 📌 Benefit: Allah opens understanding
16

Dua When Seeing Something Beautiful

مَا شَاءَ اللَّهُ لَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ
Ma sha Allah la quwwata illa billah
What Allah willed has happened, there is no power except with Allah.
⏰ When: Seeing beauty, your own or others | 📌 Benefit: Protection from evil eye
17

Dua for Forgiveness - Sayyidul Istighfar

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ خَلَقْتَنِي
Allahumma anta rabbi la ilaha illa anta khalaqtani
O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no god but You, You created me...
⏰ When: Morning and evening | 📌 Benefit: If die that day/night, Jannah guaranteed
18

Dua When Stressed/Worried

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ
Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa Huwa alayhi tawakkaltu
Allah is sufficient for me, there is no god but Him, upon Him I rely.
⏰ When: Feeling anxiety, stress | 📌 Benefit: Allah becomes sufficient for all your needs
19

Dua Before Exam/Test

رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي
Rabbi ishrah li sadri wa yassir li amri
My Lord, expand my chest for me and make my task easy for me.
⏰ When: Before exam, interview | 📌 Benefit: Allah removes difficulty, grants clarity
20

Dua When It Rains

اللَّهُمَّ صَيِّبًا نَافِعًا
Allahumma sayyiban nafi'a
O Allah, make it a beneficial rain.
⏰ When: When rain starts | 📌 Benefit: Dua during rain is accepted
21-25

Quick Daily Duas

21. Sneezing: Alhamdulillah | Reply: Yarhamukallah

22. Before Wudu: Bismillah

23. After Wudu: Ashhadu an la ilaha illallah wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan abduhu wa rasooluh

24. When Meeting Muslim: Assalamu alaikum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh

25. When Someone Says Thank You: Wa antum fa jazakumullahu khairan

26-30

Protection Duas

26. Ayatul Kursi: Before sleep and after prayer - protection from Shaytan all night

27. Last 3 Quls: Surah Ikhlas, Falaq, Nas - 3 times morning & evening

28. Entering Market: La ilaha illallahu wahdahu la shareeka lah - 1 million good deeds written

29. When in Fear: Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-hammi wal-hazan

30. For Children: U'eedhuka bikalimatillahit-tammati min kulli shaytanin wa hamma

31-35

Gratitude & Blessing Duas

31. New Clothes: Alhamdulillahil-ladhi kasani hadha wa razaqanihi min ghayri hawlin minni wa la quwwah

32. Seeing New Moon: Allahu Akbar, Allahumma ahillahu alayna bil-amni wal-iman

33. After Adhan: Allahumma rabba hadhihid-da'watit-tammah salli ala Muhammad

34. When Receiving Gift: JazakAllahu khairan

35. When Giving Gift: May Allah bless it for you

36-40

Special Situation Duas

36. Breaking Fast: Allahumma laka sumtu wa bika amantu wa alayka tawakkaltu wa ala rizqika aftartu

37. After Salah: Astaghfirullah 3x, Allahumma antas-salam wa minkas-salam

38. When in Debt: Allahumma akfini bihalalika an haramik wa aghnini bifadlika amman siwak

39. For Deceased: Allahumma ighfir lahu warhamhu wa 'afihi wa'fu anhu

40. General Comprehensive Dua: Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanah wa fil-akhirati hasanah wa qina 'adhaban-nar

How to Memorize These Duas Easily - 5 Tips

1. 5 Duas per Week: Don't try all 40 at once. Learn 5, practice daily for 7 days, then next 5. 2. Sticky Notes: Write dua and stick on bathroom mirror, fridge, car dashboard. 3. Audio Repetition: Listen to Qari reciting dua 10 times daily while commuting. 4. Teach Kids: When you teach children, you memorize faster. 5. Set Phone Alarm: "Dua after eating" alarm reminds you until it becomes habit.

Conclusion: Turn Your Day into Constant Worship

Imagine: You wake up with dua, eat with dua, work with dua, leave home with dua, return with dua, sleep with dua. 24 hours become dhikr. This is life of Prophet's companions. They didn't do extra worship – they turned daily actions into worship through duas.

Start today. Pick Dua #1 and #3. Recite when waking up and before eating. After 1 week it becomes automatic. Then add next duas. In 2 months you will know all 40 and your connection with Allah will transform completely.
