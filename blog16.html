Introduction: Ask Before You Enroll - We Believe in Transparency

Starting Quran classes is big decision for you and your family. You probably have 20 questions in mind: fees, timing, teacher quality, results, technology. We collected 20 most asked questions from 5000+ students and parents and answered them honestly here. No sugar-coating, no marketing talk – just clear answers.

If your question is not here, WhatsApp us anytime. Our admin replies within 2 hours, 7 days/week. Let's clear all doubts so you can start Quran journey with full confidence.

"So ask the people of knowledge if you do not know."
Surah An-Nahl, Ayah 43

Islam teaches us to ask questions. Don't hesitate. Here are answers:

1. Do I need to know Arabic before joining?
No, absolutely not. We have complete beginners course starting from Arabic alphabet Noorani Qaida. 40% of our students start from zero. Teacher teaches letter by letter, sound by sound. Adults age 40+ also learn successfully. If you can read A-B-C, you can learn Arabic letters too.
2. What age can my child start Quran classes?
Age 4 and above. For age 4-6 we have "Play & Learn Quran" with games, stories, coloring. 20-minute classes. For age 7+ we have standard 30-minute classes. For teenagers 13-16 we have separate batches with mature teaching style. We match teacher personality to child age.
3. Are classes really one-to-one or group?
100% one-to-one guaranteed. Only you and teacher on Zoom. No other students, no waiting time, no distraction. Teacher focuses only on YOUR recitation and mistakes. This is why we charge slightly more than group class academies – but results are 3x faster.
4. What if my child is shy and won't speak to teacher?
Very common concern. Our teachers are specially trained for shy kids. First 3 classes are "friendship building" – no pressure, just games and talking. Teacher uses puppets, stories, drawing to make child comfortable. 95% shy kids start talking by week 2. Parents can sit nearby for first 2 weeks for confidence.
5. What are your fees and payment method?
Fees start from $60/month for 5 classes. Hifz program $120/month for 20 classes. No registration fee, no book fee, no hidden charges. Payment via PayPal, credit card, bank transfer, Western Union. Pay monthly only, no yearly lock-in. Cancel anytime with 7-day notice. Sibling discount 25%.
6. Can I change class timing if my schedule changes?
Yes, anytime. Just inform admin 24 hours before. We have teachers available 6AM to 11PM, 7 days. Shift worker? We adjust monthly. Traveling? We arrange class from your location. Flexibility is our specialty because we understand modern life is busy.
7. How do I know my child is progressing?
Every month you get detailed progress report via WhatsApp + email: Pages completed, Tajweed mistakes improved, behavior notes, next month targets. Plus teacher sends voice note after each class summarizing what was taught. You are always in loop, not in dark.
8. What technology do I need for online classes?
Just smartphone, laptop, or tablet with internet and Zoom app. That's it. We guide you step-by-step to install Zoom. Internet speed 2MB is enough. If internet disconnects, teacher calls you back immediately and extends class time. Technical issues are our problem, not yours.
9. Are your teachers qualified and native Arabic speakers?
All teachers have Ijazah certification – unbroken chain to Prophet (PBUH). 70% are native Arabic speakers from Egypt, Saudi, Pakistan. All speak fluent English. We verify credentials, test Tajweed, and train them in online teaching psychology before hiring. You can request teacher profile before starting.
10. Can I request male or female teacher for my daughter?
Yes, 100%. We have 50+ female teachers for girls and ladies who prefer female teacher. Just mention preference during registration. For boys under 12, both male/female teachers available. For boys 13+, male teacher assigned. Your comfort and Islamic guidelines are respected.
11. What if I miss a class due to emergency?
Inform us 3 hours before and we reschedule at free. If emergency happens during class, teacher adjusts next class time and covers missed portion. We understand life happens – illness, travel, family events. We are flexible, not strict. One free makeup class per month included.
12. How long does it take to complete Quran/Hifz?
Depends on student effort. Quran reading with Tajweed: 6-12 months for 30 min, 5 days/week. Hifz: 2-4 years for 1 page daily. Adult Hifz: 3-5 years. We never give false promise "Hifz in 1 year" because that causes burnout. Our 1-page daily method is sustainable and 85% students complete vs 30% industry average.
13. Do you provide certificate after completion?
Yes, official completion certificate with student name, course details, teacher signature, and academy seal. For Hifz students, we arrange Ijazah test with senior Qari. Digital certificate sent within 7 days. Hard copy mailed on request. Certificate recognized by Islamic organizations worldwide.
14. Can adults join or is it only for kids?
40% of our students are adults age 18-70. We have special "Adult Quran Course" with patient teachers who understand adult psychology. No embarrassment, no comparison with kids. Separate batches, flexible timing after work hours. Many adults start at age 45+ and complete successfully.
15. What about 3 free trial classes – are they really free?
100% free, no credit card required, no obligation. You get 3 full 30-minute classes with Ijazah teacher. Experience teaching style, platform, timing. After 3 classes, if you like it, enroll. If not, thank you and goodbye. 94% students enroll after trial because they see quality difference.
16. Do you teach only Quran or other Islamic subjects too?
We teach complete package: Quran with Tajweed, Hifz, Noorani Qaida, Translation & Tafseer, Arabic Language, Islamic Studies for Kids, 40 Duas, Seerah. You can combine courses. Example: Child learns Quran 3 days + Islamic Studies 2 days weekly. One academy, all Islamic education needs covered.
17. How do you ensure safety for kids online?
Safety is top priority. All teachers background-checked. Classes recorded with parent permission for quality check. Parents can sit in class anytime. Female teachers available for girls. No personal contact between teacher and student – only through academy Zoom and admin WhatsApp. We follow strict child protection policy.
18. What if I am not satisfied after enrolling?
30-day satisfaction guarantee. If within first month you feel teaching style doesn't match, we change teacher free or refund unused classes, no questions asked. But honestly, only 2% students ask for change because we match teacher to student personality during trial classes.
19. Do you offer discount for multiple children/family?
Yes! Sibling discount 25% for 2nd child, 40% for 3rd child. Family package: 4 family members learning = 40% off total. We believe entire family should benefit from Quran, not just one child. Discount applies automatically, no coupon needed.
20. How do I start registration process?
Super simple: 1) Fill form below or WhatsApp us, 2) We call you within 2 hours to understand needs, 3) Schedule 3 FREE trial classes at your convenient time, 4) After trial, if happy, choose package and start regular classes. No paperwork, no long forms. Start within 24-48 hours of first contact.
