Benefits of Memorizing Surah Yaseen
Heart of Quran - 8 Amazing Virtues & Rewards Backed by Hadith
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 16 Min Read 📚 Surah Yaseen ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Why Surah Yaseen is Called "Heart of Quran"

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "Everything has a heart, and the heart of Quran is Surah Yaseen." Just like heart pumps blood to whole body, Surah Yaseen gives life to your Iman. 83 verses, 10 pages only, but benefits equal to reading 10 parts of Quran according to some narrations.

Muslims worldwide recite Yaseen for sick, for deceased, for Hajat, for protection. But do we know WHY? What exactly happens when you memorize and recite Surah Yaseen daily? This article explains 8 authentic benefits backed by Hadith and scholar opinions. By end, you will memorize at least first 10 verses InshaAllah.

يس ۝ وَالْقُرْآنِ الْحَكِيمِ ۝ إِنَّكَ لَمِنَ الْمُرْسَلِينَ
"Ya Seen. By the wise Quran. Indeed you, [O Muhammad], are from among the messengers."
Surah Yaseen, Ayat 1-3

Allah Himself starts Surah by swearing on Quran and confirming Prophet's status. This shows importance. Let's explore 8 benefits.

1

Benefit 1: Equals Reading 10 Times Quran

Hadith: "Whoever recites Surah Yaseen once, Allah rewards him as if he recited Quran 10 times." Tirmidhi. Scholars explain: This doesn't mean you skip rest of Quran. It means barakah and reward multiplier. In 10 minutes you get reward of 2 hours recitation.

For busy people: No time for 1 Juz? Read Yaseen. 10 pages = reward of 100 pages. Allah's mercy is limitless. This is special gift for Ummah Muhammad (PBUH).

2

Benefit 2: Forgiveness of Sins Before Death

Hadith: "Whoever recites Surah Yaseen seeking Allah's pleasure, his past sins are forgiven." If person reads Yaseen regularly, Allah cleans his record. Imagine dying with clean slate – all sins erased just because you loved this Surah.

Practice: Read Yaseen after Maghrib with intention: "Ya Allah forgive my sins." Make it daily habit. Small deed, massive forgiveness.

3

Benefit 3: Ease at Time of Death - Sakarat Relief

Scholars and experience show: Person who memorizes Yaseen gets ease during Sakaratul Maut – pain of death. Angels recite Yaseen near dying person, Shaytan cannot approach, soul leaves body peacefully.

Hadith: Recite Yaseen for dying person
Prophet (PBUH) said: "Recite Surah Yaseen upon your dying ones." Abu Dawud

Memorize it now so when your time comes, angels recite from YOUR heart, not just book.

4

Benefit 4: Fulfillment of All Hajat/Needs

Hadith: "Whoever recites Yaseen at beginning of day, Allah fulfills all his needs for that day." Many scholars call Yaseen "Quran of Hajat". Stuck in job, marriage, health issue, debt? Read Yaseen after Fajr with specific dua.

Method: After Fajr, read Yaseen, then make dua: "Ya Allah, by barakah of Yaseen, solve my problem of..." 40 days test it. Countless people report miracles.

5

Benefit 5: Protection from Shaytan and Evil Eye

Surah Yaseen has powerful verses that drive away Shaytan. Verses 7-12 describe how Allah sealed hearts of disbelievers – same protection works for believer. Reciting daily creates spiritual shield around home.

For home: Play Yaseen recitation loud daily. Shaytan cannot stay where Yaseen is recited. Children protected from nightmares, evil eye, jinn disturbance.

6

Benefit 6: Intercession on Day of Judgment

Surah Yaseen itself will come as person on Qiyamah and argue with Allah: "Ya Allah, this servant recited me daily, forgive him." Can you imagine Surah talking to Allah for YOU? This is love between Quran and its reciter.

Invest now: Every verse you memorize is lawyer for you in court of Allah. 83 verses = 83 witnesses for your forgiveness.

7

Benefit 7: Light in Grave - Qabr ki Roshni

Hadith: "Surah Yaseen is called 'Muni'ah' – the Preventer. It prevents punishment of grave." Person who dies while reciting Yaseen, his grave becomes garden from gardens of Jannah. Darkness turns to light.

Question: Grave is either garden of Jannah or pit of Hell. Yaseen decides. 10 pages memorize karo, lifetime peace in grave.

8

Benefit 8: Understanding Tawheed & Akhirah

Yaseen contains entire Islam in 83 verses: Story of 3 messengers to village, resurrection of dead, proof of Allah's power through nature, warning for disbelievers, glad tidings for believers. Memorize Yaseen = memorize summary of Quran.

For new Muslims: Scholars tell them "Memorize Yaseen first" because it teaches Aqeedah, Akhlaq, Akhirah in one Surah. Perfect starting point.

How to Memorize Surah Yaseen in 40 Days

83 verses ÷ 40 days = 2 verses daily only. 5 minutes work. Method: 1) Listen to Qari 10 times, 2) Read 2 verses 20 times looking, 3) Close Mushaf and recite 10 times, 4) Revise yesterday's 2 verses. After 40 days you know whole Yaseen.

Best time: After Fajr. Mind fresh, barakah maximum. Pair with friend for motivation.

Daily Routine: When to Read Yaseen

1. After Fajr: For daily Hajat fulfillment. 2. After Maghrib: For forgiveness of sins. 3. Thursday night: For deceased parents. 4. When sick: For shifa. 5. Before sleep: For protection all night.

Common Questions About Yaseen

Q: Can women read Yaseen during periods? A: Yes, from memory or phone, without touching Mushaf. Reading Quran is allowed, only touching physical Mushaf prohibited.

Q: Is "Yaseen khwani" for dead bidah? A: Scholars differ. Majority allow sending reward/thawab to deceased by reciting Quran. Intention matters – do it for Allah, not show.

Conclusion: Make Yaseen Your Daily Companion

10 pages only. If you read 1 page daily, Yaseen completes in 10 days. If you memorize 2 verses daily, complete in 40 days. But benefits last lifetime + after death.

Prophet loved Yaseen. Sahaba memorized it first after Fatiha. Don't delay. Start today with verse 1-2. Repeat 20 times. Tomorrow verse 3-4. After 2 months you will be "Yaseen wala" – person known for this blessed Surah.
