Power of Bismillah in Daily Life
19 Letters, Infinite Barakah - How One Sentence Changes Everything
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 14 Min Read 📚 Daily Adhkar ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: The First Verse of Quran

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ - "In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Especially Merciful." Just 19 Arabic letters, but every Surah starts with it except Taubah. Every Prophet started work with it. Every meal, every journey, every task begins with it.

We say Bismillah 100 times daily without thinking. But do we know the power? Scholars say: "If mountains knew barakah of Bismillah, they would move from their places." This 3-word sentence connects you directly to Allah before every action. Let's unlock 10 powers of Bismillah you never knew.

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
اقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ
"Read in the name of your Lord who created."
Surah Al-Alaq, Ayah 1

First revelation was "Iqra Bismi Rabbik" – Read with Allah's name. Not just read, but read WITH Allah. That's Bismillah's secret.

1

Power 1: Protection from Shaytan

Hadith: "When servant says Bismillah, Shaytan becomes small like fly. When he forgets, Shaytan inflates like house." Before eating, Shaytan wants to eat with you. Say Bismillah – he runs away hungry.

Daily use: Before entering bathroom, before sleeping, before leaving home. 19 letters = invisible shield. Shaytan cannot harm what starts with Allah's name.

2

Power 2: Barakah in Rizq and Food

Food without Bismillah has no barakah. You eat full plate but stomach empty after 1 hour. Food with Bismillah – small quantity satisfies, gives energy, becomes medicine.

Science + Deen: When you say Bismillah with intention, your mind becomes grateful. Grateful mind digests better. Barakah is not magic – it's Allah putting benefit in small things.

3

Power 3: Success in All Work

Any work started without Allah = half success, full stress. Work started with Bismillah = Allah becomes your partner. Business deal, exam, interview, marriage – say Bismillah first.

Story: Imam Shafi said: "I never wrote one line without Bismillah. That's why my books have barakah 1200 years later." Your work becomes Sadaqah Jariyah when done with Allah's name.

4

Power 4: 786 Secret - Numerical Value

Bismillah letters = Ba+Seen+Meem+Alif+Lam+Lam+Ha = 786. That's why Muslims write 786 instead of Bismillah on letters, shops. Not innovation – it's respect. When you can't write full Arabic, 786 reminds you of Allah.

Note: 786 is not magic number. Power is in Allah's name, not digits. But using 786 keeps you remembering Bismillah when full sentence not possible.

5

Power 5: Protection from Fire of Hell

Hadith: "Bismillah has 19 letters. Hell has 19 angels guarding it. Whoever says Bismillah sincerely, Allah saves him from each angel by each letter." 19 letters = 19 layers of protection.

For children: Teach kids Bismillah before sleeping. Tell them "19 angels guard you whole night." Kids feel safe, sleep peacefully. Deen + psychology together.

6

Power 6: Every Action Becomes Ibadah

Eating is mubah – neutral act. Eating with Bismillah becomes worship. Driving to work is worldly. Driving with Bismillah becomes jihad. This is Islam's beauty – turn daily life into reward factory.

Rule: Intention + Bismillah = Ibadah. Without Bismillah = just habit. With Bismillah = Allah's reward.

7

Power 7: Key to All Doors

Prophet Sulaiman's letter to Queen Saba started with Bismillah – and whole kingdom submitted. When you start with Allah's name, closed doors open. Job rejection turns to acceptance. Sick person gets shifa.

Practice: Stuck in problem? Write Bismillah 786 times, make dua. Not because number is magic, but because 786 times you remembered Allah. Allah loves persistent caller.

8

Power 8: Meaning Breakdown - 3 Names, 3 Worlds

Allah: Personal name of God. No one else can be called Allah. Means "The One worthy of worship".

Ar-Rahman: Mercy for ALL creation – Muslim, kafir, animal, insect. Rain falls on everyone. That's Rahman.

Ar-Rahim: Special mercy ONLY for believers in Akhirah. Jannah is Rahim. When you say Bismillah, you ask for both mercies – worldly + hereafter.

When MUST You Say Bismillah?

1. Before eating/drinking 2. Before sleeping 3. Before entering bathroom 4. Before leaving home 5. Before starting car 6. Before writing/typing 7. Before intimacy with spouse 8. Before slaughtering animal 9. Before starting any work 10. Before reciting Quran

Rule: If Prophet did it, we do it. He said Bismillah before EVERYTHING.

3 Common Mistakes We Make

1. Saying fast without heart: "Bismillah" should come from tongue AND heart. Feel Allah is watching you. 2. Forgetting mid-way: Forgot before eating? Say "Bismillahi awwalahu wa akhirah" in middle. 3. Saying only for food: Bismillah is for ALL actions, not just meal.

How to Teach Kids Bismillah

Make it song: "Bismillah, Bismillah, Allah ka naam lein". Reward sticker every time child says before eating. After 21 days, it becomes automatic. My student age 4 says Bismillah before cartoons – that's tarbiyah!

Wall hanging, fridge sticker, car dashboard – keep Bismillah visible. Eyes see = tongue remembers.

Conclusion: 19 Letters Can Change Your Life

Test it for 7 days. Say Bismillah before EVERYTHING consciously. Note changes: Less stress, more barakah in time, problems solve easier, heart feels peaceful. You will never forget Bismillah again.

Prophet said: "All important work without Bismillah is cut off from barakah." Don't let your work be "cut off". Start every email, every call, every step with Bismillah.

"Bismillah is password to Allah's help" – Make it your habit today!
