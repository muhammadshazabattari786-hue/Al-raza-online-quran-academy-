Morning & Evening Azkar
Fortress of Muslim - 15 Essential Daily Supplications for Protection
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 17 Min Read 📚 Hisnul Muslim ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Your Daily Spiritual Armor

You lock car, lock home, install CCTV for physical security. But what about spiritual security? Shaytan, evil eye, jinn, anxiety, stress – these attack daily. Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) gave us "Hisnul Muslim" – Fortress of Muslim through specific Azkar morning and evening.

Just 10 minutes after Fajr and Maghrib, and you are under Allah's protection for 12 hours. Sahaba never missed these Azkar even in battle. This article covers 15 most essential Azkar with Arabic, transliteration, meaning, and benefit. Print this and keep near prayer mat.

فَاذْكُرُونِي أَذْكُرْكُمْ وَاشْكُرُوا لِي وَلَا تَكْفُرُونِ
"So remember Me; I will remember you. And be grateful to Me and do not deny Me."
Surah Al-Baqarah, Ayah 152

Allah promises: You remember Me morning/evening, I remember you and protect you. Deal is clear. Let's start with morning Azkar.

1

Azkar 1: Ayatul Kursi - Verse of Throne

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ...
Allahu la ilaha illa Huwal-Hayyul-Qayyum...
Allah - there is no god except Him, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer...
🛡️ Benefit: Recite after Fajr + Maghrib + before sleep. Protection from Shaytan till next prayer. No harm can reach you.
2

Azkar 2: Last 3 Quls - Surah Ikhlas, Falaq, Nas

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ... قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ... قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ
Qul Huwallahu Ahad... Qul a'udhu birabbil-falaq... Qul a'udhu birabbin-nas
Say, He is Allah, One... Say, I seek refuge in Lord of dawn... Say, I seek refuge in Lord of mankind
🛡️ Benefit: Recite 3 times morning/evening. Blow on hands and wipe body. Protection from magic, evil eye, jinn, all harm.
3

Azkar 3: Sayyidul Istighfar - Master of Forgiveness

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ خَلَقْتَنِي...
Allahumma anta rabbi la ilaha illa anta khalaqtani...
O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no god but You, You created me...
🛡️ Benefit: If die morning/evening after reciting, Jannah guaranteed. Best dua for forgiveness.
4

Azkar 4: 4 Times - Razi'tu Billahi Rabba

رَضِيتُ بِاللَّهِ رَبًّا وَبِالْإِسْلَامِ دِينًا وَبِمُحَمَّدٍ نَبِيًّا
Radheetu billahi rabban wa bil-islami deenan wa bi Muhammadin nabiyya
I am pleased with Allah as Lord, Islam as religion, Muhammad as Prophet
🛡️ Benefit: 3 times morning/evening. Allah promises to make you pleased on Qiyamah. Strengthens Iman.
5

Azkar 5: Hasbiyallahu - Allah is Sufficient

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ
Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa Huwa alayhi tawakkaltu
Allah is sufficient for me, there is no god but Him, upon Him I rely
🛡️ Benefit: 7 times morning/evening. Allah takes care of all your worries, stress, needs for that day.
6

Azkar 6: 100 Times - SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ
SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi
Glory be to Allah and praise Him
🛡️ Benefit: 100 times morning/evening. Sins forgiven even if like foam of sea. Light on tongue, heavy on scales.
7

Azkar 7: 10 Times - La ilaha illAllah

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ
La ilaha illAllahu wahdahu la shareeka lah
There is no god but Allah alone, no partner has He
🛡️ Benefit: 10 times morning/evening. Equivalent to freeing 4 slaves, 10 good deeds written, 10 sins erased, protected from Shaytan whole day.
8

Azkar 8: 3 Times - Allahumma inni as'alukal-afiyah

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَافِيَةَ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ
Allahumma inni as'alukal-'afiyata fid-dunya wal-akhirah
O Allah, I ask You for well-being in this world and Hereafter
🛡️ Benefit: 3 times morning/evening. 'Afiyah = protection from all illness, calamity, fitnah. Best comprehensive dua.
9

Azkar 9: Morning Dua - Asbahna wa asbahal mulk

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ
Asbahna wa asbahal-mulku lillah
We have reached morning and dominion belongs to Allah
🛡️ Benefit: Morning only. Acknowledges Allah's control. Start day with Tawheed consciousness.
10

Azkar 10: Evening Dua - Amsayna wa amsal mulk

أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ
Amsayna wa amsal-mulku lillah
We have reached evening and dominion belongs to Allah
🛡️ Benefit: Evening only. End day remembering Allah owns everything. Protection through night.
11

Azkar 11: Protection Dua - A'udhu bikalimatillah

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
A'udhu bikalimatillahit-tammati min sharri ma khalaq
I seek refuge in Allah's perfect words from evil of what He created
🛡️ Benefit: 3 times evening. No poison, no sting, no harm will affect you that night. Powerful protection.
12

Azkar 12: 3 Times - Bismillahil-ladhi la yadurr

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ
Bismillahil-ladhi la yadurru ma'asmihi shay'un
In name of Allah with whose name nothing harms in earth or heaven
🛡️ Benefit: 3 times morning/evening. Complete protection from sudden calamity, accident, illness whole day/night.
13

Azkar 13: Morning - Allahumma bika asbahna

اللَّهُمَّ بِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا
Allahumma bika asbahna wa bika amsayna
O Allah, by You we reach morning and by You we reach evening
🛡️ Benefit: Morning. Hand over control of day to Allah. Live day knowing He is managing everything.
14

Azkar 14: Evening - Allahumma bika amsayna

اللَّهُمَّ بِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا وَبِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا
Allahumma bika amsayna wa bika asbahna
O Allah, by You we reach evening and by You we reach morning
🛡️ Benefit: Evening. Trust Allah with night. Sleep peacefully knowing He guards you.
15

Azkar 15: 33x SubhanAllah, 33x Alhamdulillah, 34x Allahu Akbar

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ ٣، الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ ٣، اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ ٣٤
SubhanAllah 33x, Alhamdulillah 33x, Allahu Akbar 34x
Glory be to Allah, All praise to Allah, Allah is Greatest
🛡️ Benefit: After every prayer + morning/evening. Fatima (RA) was taught this by Prophet. Gives strength for whole day, no fatigue.

Perfect Timing: When to Recite

Morning Azkar: After Fajr till sunrise. Best time: 10 min after Fajr while sitting on prayer mat.

Evening Azkar: After Asr till Maghrib. Best time: 10 min before Maghrib.

Total time: 10 minutes only. 5 min morning + 5 min evening = 24 hour protection.

30-Day Chart Method for Consistency

Conclusion: Don't Leave Home Without Your Armor

Soldier never goes to battlefield without armor. You face Shaytan, stress, evil eye daily – Azkar is your armor. 15 Azkar, 10 minutes, lifetime protection.

Start tomorrow Fajr. Read only Ayatul Kursi + 3 Quls + Sayyidul Istighfar. 3 minutes. Add one more each week. After 2 months you know all 15 by heart.
