Importance of Salah for Muslims
5 Daily Prayers - First Thing Judged & Key to Paradise
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 18 Min Read 📚 Salah ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Salah - The Backbone of Islam

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "The first thing for which a servant will be brought to account on Day of Judgment is Salah. If it is sound, all his deeds will be sound. If it is corrupt, all his deeds will be corrupt." Tirmidhi.

Allah gave us 5 pillars. Salah is the pillar you perform 5 times daily, 35 times weekly, 1825 times yearly. Zakat once yearly, Hajj once lifetime, Roza 30 days yearly. But Salah? Every single day. Why? Because without Salah, Islam collapses like building without pillar.

This article explains why Salah is non-negotiable, what happens when we pray, what happens when we miss, and 7 practical tips to never miss prayer again. Even if you pray sometimes, read this – it will change your relationship with Sajdah forever.

إِنَّ الصَّلَاةَ كَانَتْ عَلَى الْمُؤْمِنِينَ كِتَابًا مَّوْقُوتًا
"Indeed, prayer has been decreed upon the believers a decree of specified times."
Surah An-Nisa, Ayah 103

Allah didn't say "pray when free". He said "specified times". Fajr has time, Zuhr has time. This teaches discipline. Let's understand 8 reasons Salah is life.

1

Reason 1: Direct Connection - 5 Times Meeting with Allah

5 times daily Allah calls: "Hayya alas-Salah" – Come to prayer. You leave work, phone, family, and stand before King of Kings. No appointment needed, no gatekeeper. Just "Allahu Akbar" and you are talking to Allah.

Imagine: President calls you 5 times daily for private meeting. You will cancel everything. Allah, Lord of universe, calls you. What excuse do we have? Salah is honor, not burden.

2

Reason 2: Washes Sins 5 Times Daily

Hadith: Salah washes sins like river
Prophet (PBUH) said: "If there was river at your door, you bathed 5 times daily, would any dirt remain?" Sahaba said No. Prophet said: "Salah is like that river, washes away sins."

Made mistake at 9AM? Zuhr at 1PM wipes it. Angry at 3PM? Asr at 4PM cleans heart. Sleep with sins? Isha + Fajr purify you. Salah is daily reset button for soul.

3

Reason 3: Difference Between Muslim & Kafir

Hadith: "What stands between servant and kufr is abandoning Salah." Sahih Muslim. Scholars have 2 opinions: Missing Salah intentionally = kufr. Missing lazily = major sin close to kufr.

Reality check: We say "La ilaha illAllah" but don't stand for Allah 5 times. Is that true faith? Salah is practical proof of Kalima. Tongue says Muslim, body must prove it in Sajdah.

4

Reason 4: Protection from Fahsha and Munkar

إِنَّ الصَّلَاةَ تَنْهَىٰ عَنِ الْفَحْشَاءِ وَالْمُنكَرِ
"Indeed, prayer prohibits immorality and wrongdoing."
Surah Al-Ankabut, Ayah 45

True Salah stops you from lying, backbiting, haram relationship, interest. If Salah doesn't stop sin, check Salah – maybe you are just doing exercise, not praying. Quality Salah = changed character.

5

Reason 5: Light in Grave & On Sirat Bridge

Hadith: "Salah is light." On Qiyamah when it's dark, people with Salah will have light from their Sajdah marks. On Sirat bridge thinner than hair, Salah will be boat carrying you across Hell to Jannah.

Question: Grave is dark for 1000s of years. Want light? Sajdah now. Want boat on Sirat? Ruku now. Investment is 5-10 minutes, return is eternity.

6

Reason 6: Was Gifted in Miraj - Direct from Allah

Zakat came via Jibreel. Quran came via Jibreel. But Salah? Allah called Prophet to 7th heaven and gave Salah directly, no angel in between. 50 prayers reduced to 5, but reward of 50. Allah wanted to give us maximum reward for minimum effort.

Respect: Gift given by King Himself should be valued most. We miss Salah like gift from Allah has no value. Astaghfirullah.

7

Reason 7: Mental & Physical Health Benefits

Science now confirms: Ruku = blood flow to brain. Sajdah = oxygen to brain, reduces stress. Sequence of movements = perfect yoga. Standing 5 times = exercise for legs/back. Wudu 5 times = hygiene + skin benefits.

Deen + Dunya: Allah ordered Salah 1400 years ago. Now doctors prescribe "mindfulness + exercise" for depression. We had solution already. Salah is therapy for anxiety, depression, overthinking.

8

Reason 8: Last Words of Prophet - "Salah, Salah"

When Prophet was dying, he kept repeating: "As-Salah, As-Salah, and what your right hands possess." Even in last breath, he worried about Ummah abandoning Salah. If Prophet cared so much, why don't we?

Story: Bilal (RA) tortured, stones on chest, but when asked "Stop Salah?" he said "Ahad, Ahad" – Allah is One. We have AC, soft carpet, still "I'm busy". Compare your excuse to Bilal's torture.

7 Tips to Never Miss Salah Again

1. Alarm with Azan: Set phone alarm 5 min before prayer with Muazzin voice. 2. Prayer mat visible: Keep mat in room, not cupboard. Eyes see = heart remembers. 3. Buddy system: Friend texts "Asr?" daily. 4. Start small: If 5 prayers hard, start with 2 – Fajr + Maghrib. Add one each week. 5. Understand meaning: Learn translation of Al-Fatiha. When you understand "Iyaka na'budu", prayer becomes conversation. 6. Repent + restart: Missed Zuhr? Don't quit. Pray Asr on time. Allah loves returner. 7. Remember death: This might be last Salah. Pray like Janazah prayer.

For Beginners: Can't Pray Full Yet?

Start with just Fatiha + short Surah. No problem. Stand, bow, prostrate – even if mistakes. Allah sees effort, not perfection. Learn gradually. Our academy teaches "Salah for beginners" course – 10 days, you learn step by step with English explanation.

Teach Kids Salah Early

Prophet said: "Command children to pray at 7, beat lightly at 10 if they don't." Not beating, but discipline. Make prayer game: "Who does longest Sajdah gets chocolate". Reward, don't force. Age 4-5 child can imitate you. Age 10 it should be habit.

Conclusion: Salah is Not Burden, It's Gift

We think "I have to pray". Change thinking to "I GET to pray". 5 meetings with Allah daily. Who gets this honor? Kings? Billionaires? No – every Muslim, poor or rich, gets direct access to Allah 5 times.

Next Azan you hear, don't say "5 more minutes". Say "Labbaik Ya Allah" – I am coming. Run to prayer. That run will be witnessed by angels and recorded as Jihad.
