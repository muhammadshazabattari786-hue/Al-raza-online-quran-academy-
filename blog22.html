Virtues of Friday - Jummah Mubarak
Best Day of Week - 10 Special Deeds & Rewards for Muslims
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 16 Min Read 📚 Jummah ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Why Friday is Called "Sayyidul Ayyam"

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "The best day the sun rises upon is Friday. On it Adam was created, on it he was entered into Paradise, on it he was expelled, and the Hour will not come except on Friday." Tirmidhi.

Friday is Eid for Muslims weekly. While Christians have Sunday, Jews have Saturday, Allah chose Friday for Ummah Muhammad. Angels stand at mosque doors recording names of those who come early. Dua made on Friday has guaranteed acceptance time. Yet many Muslims treat Friday like normal day.

This article covers 10 special virtues of Jummah and exact deeds you must do from Thursday night to Friday night. Miss Friday = miss golden opportunity every week. Let's maximize this blessed day.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا نُودِيَ لِلصَّلَاةِ مِن يَوْمِ الْجُمُعَةِ فَاسْعَوْا إِلَىٰ ذِكْرِ اللَّهِ
"O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah."
Surah Al-Jumu'ah, Ayah 9

Allah commands: Leave business, leave work, run to Jummah. When Creator of universe says "run", we say "I'm busy". Let's fix that.

1

Virtue 1: Adam (AS) Created & Entered Jannah on Friday

Friday is birthday of humanity. Allah created Adam (AS) on Friday, taught him names, made angels prostrate. Same day he entered Jannah. When you attend Jummah, you connect to origin of human story.

Lesson: Friday is day of new beginnings. Make taubah Friday, start new habit Friday. Barakah of Adam's creation is in this day.

2

Virtue 2: Hour of Acceptance - Dua Never Rejected

Hadith: Hour on Friday
Prophet (PBUH) said: "There is an hour on Friday when Muslim asks Allah for good, Allah gives it." Bukhari, Muslim

Scholars differ which hour: Last hour before Maghrib, time between Khutbah and Iqama, after Asr. Best strategy: Make dua whole day, especially after Asr till Maghrib.

Action: Prepare dua list Thursday night. On Friday after Asr, sit on prayer mat, cry to Allah. That 1 hour > 1000 nights of dua other days.

3

Virtue 3: Sins Forgiven Between Two Fridays

Hadith: "Whoever bathes Friday, comes early, listens to Khutbah without talking, then prays, his sins between this Friday and last Friday are forgiven." Muslim.

Math: 7 days sins = wiped in 30 minutes. Ghibat, lies, angry, missed prayers – all gone if you follow 4 conditions: Bath, early, listen, no talking.

4

Virtue 4: Angels Record Names at Mosque Door

Hadith: "On Friday, angels stand at every door of mosque recording people in order of arrival. When Imam sits for Khutbah, they close records and listen." Bukhari.

Competition: First person = reward of camel sacrifice. Second = cow. Third = sheep. Fourth = chicken. Fifth = egg. Arrive 30 min early, get camel reward. Arrive late = maybe egg or nothing. Angels are watching YOU.

5

Virtue 5: Dying Friday = Protection from Grave Punishment

Hadith: "Any Muslim who dies Friday, Allah protects him from trial of grave." Tirmidhi. Death on Friday is sign of good ending. Allah honors His servant by taking soul on best day.

Hope: If you have good deeds Friday, Allah might choose that day for you. Live every Friday like last Friday.

6

Virtue 6: Surah Kahf Protection from Dajjal

Hadith: "Whoever reads Surah Kahf on Friday, light will shine for him between two Fridays. Whoever reads last 10 verses, protected from Dajjal." Hakim.

Method: Read Surah Kahf Friday morning or any time before Maghrib. 18th Juz, 110 verses. Takes 20-30 min. Light for whole week + protection from biggest fitnah Dajjal.

7

Virtue 7: Sending Durood - Prophet Hears You Directly

Hadith: "Increase Durood on me Friday because your Durood is presented to me." Abu Dawud. Other days angels deliver Durood. Friday? Prophet hears YOUR name and YOUR Durood directly.

Target: Send 1000 Durood Friday. "Allahumma salli ala Muhammad". Use tasbeeh counter. Prophet will say "Peace be upon you O so-and-so son of so-and-so". Imagine that honor!

8

Virtue 8: Bath, Perfume, Clean Clothes = Sunnah Reward

Ghusl Friday is wajib for men attending Jummah. Cut nails, trim mustache, wear best clothes, apply perfume. This is not for fashion – it's worship. Angels smell perfume, Shaytan runs away.

Women: Ghusl not wajib but mustahab. Prepare special Friday clothes for kids. Make Friday feel different from other days at home.

9

Virtue 9: Khutbah = 2 Rakah Replacement

Zuhr is 4 Rakah, but Friday Zuhr replaced by 2 Rakah Jummah + Khutbah. Khutbah counts as 2 Rakah. That's why talking during Khutbah is haram – you are breaking prayer.

Adab: Come before Khutbah starts. Sit close to Imam. Don't use phone. Don't sleep. Listen like Quran is being recited – because Khutbah is reminder from Allah.

10

Virtue 10: Congregation Reward x27

Hadith: "Prayer in congregation is 27 times better than prayer alone." Bukhari. Friday prayer is biggest congregation weekly. 100, 500, 1000 people praying together – Allah's mercy descends on all.

Women: Pray at home is better reward, but can attend if no fitnah. If stay home, still get Friday virtues by reading Surah Kahf + Durood + dua hour.

Friday Timeline: Perfect Schedule

Thursday night: Make dua list, set alarm early. Friday Fajr: Read Surah Sajdah + Insan. After sunrise: Read Surah Kahf. Before Zuhr: Ghusl, perfume, best clothes. 11:30 AM: Leave for mosque, get camel reward. Khutbah: Listen silently. After Asr-Maghrib: Dua hour – cry for needs.

5 Mistakes to Avoid on Friday

1. Arriving late during Khutbah. 2. Talking/phone during Khutbah. 3. Crossing people to reach front row. 4. Not bathing Ghusl. 5. Skipping Surah Kahf. Avoid these = get full reward.

Conclusion: Don't Waste Your Weekly Eid

Jummah comes 52 times yearly. 52 chances for sins forgiveness, 52 hours of dua acceptance, 52 Surah Kahf lights. Yet we waste it in sleep, market, phone.

This Friday, try full schedule. Ghusl, early, Khutbah, Kahf, Durood 1000x, dua hour. After Maghrib ask yourself: "Did my heart feel different?" Answer will be YES.

"Jummah Mubarak = Weekly reset button" – Set Friday reminder now!
