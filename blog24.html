Benefits of Fasting - Roza Virtues
Ramadan + Sunnah Fasts - Physical, Spiritual & Social Rewards
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 17 Min Read 📚 Sawm ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Fasting - The Secret Pillar of Islam

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "Allah says: Every deed of son of Adam is for him except fasting; it is for Me and I will reward it." Bukhari, Muslim. All ibadah has fixed reward 10 to 700 times. But fasting? Allah says "I will reward it" – meaning unlimited, no calculation.

Fasting is 4th pillar. 30 days yearly Ramadan + Sunnah fasts Monday-Thursday, 13-14-15, Ashura, Arafah. But most Muslims think fasting = hunger. Reality: Fasting is training camp for soul, detox for body, shield for Akhirah.

This article covers 10 benefits of fasting backed by Quran, Hadith, and science. After reading, you will love Roza not just in Ramadan but whole year InshaAllah.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ الصِّيَامُ كَمَا كُتِبَ عَلَى الَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَتَّقُونَ
"O you who have believed, decreed upon you is fasting as it was decreed upon those before you that you may become righteous."
Surah Al-Baqarah, Ayah 183

Goal of fasting is not hunger – it's Taqwa. Taqwa = Allah-consciousness. When you control stomach, you can control eyes, tongue, anger. Let's explore benefits.

1

Benefit 1: Allah's Personal Reward - No Limit

Hadith Qudsi: Fasting is for Me
Allah says: "Fasting is for Me and I will reward it. He leaves food, drink, desires for My sake." Bukhari

Salah reward 10x, Sadaqah 700x, but fasting = Allah decides reward. Maybe 1 million x? Maybe Jannah directly? Secret between you and Allah. That's why fasting is hidden ibadah – no one knows if you truly fasted or not.

2

Benefit 2: Shield from Hell Fire

Hadith: "Fasting is shield, just like shield in battle protects from sword, fasting protects from Hell fire." Also: "Whoever fasts 1 day for Allah, Allah distances his face from Hell 70 years." Tirmidhi.

Math: Fast 30 days Ramadan = 2100 years away from Hell. Fast Monday-Thursday weekly = full protection. Fasting is insurance policy for Akhirah.

3

Benefit 3: Special Gate in Jannah - Ar-Rayyan

Hadith: "In Paradise there is gate called Ar-Rayyan. Only those who fasted will enter from it. When last faster enters, gate closes." Bukhari, Muslim.

Imagine: Qiyamah day, crowd going to Jannah, but special VIP gate opens only for you because you fasted. Angels call "Where are Sa'imeen?" and you walk in with honor. All non-fasters watch with regret.

4

Benefit 4: Sins Forgiven - Ramadan Clean Slate

Hadith: "Whoever fasts Ramadan with faith and hope of reward, his past sins are forgiven." Bukhari. Also: "Between Ramadan to Ramadan is expiation for sins between them if major sins avoided." Muslim.

Annual reset: Like computer format, Ramadan formats your sin file. 11 months sins deleted in 30 days. What better deal? Fast sincerely, start fresh Shawwal 1st.

5

Benefit 5: Controls Desires - Shield for Youth

Hadith: "O young men, whoever can afford marriage should marry. Whoever cannot, let him fast, for fasting is shield." Bukhari. Hunger reduces sexual desire, anger, greed. That's why fasting prescribed for those who can't marry.

For students: Exam stress, phone addiction, bad habits – fast Monday-Thursday. 16 hours no food = 16 hours discipline training. Soul becomes strong.

6

Benefit 6: Dua Accepted While Fasting

Hadith: "Three whose dua is not rejected: fasting person until he breaks fast, just ruler, oppressed person." Tirmidhi. At Iftar time, angels say Ameen to your dua.

Strategy: Prepare dua list before Maghrib. Last 10 min before Iftar, raise hands, cry. That dua has direct line to Allah. Ask for Jannah, parents forgiveness, hidayah.

7

Benefit 7: Physical Health - Body Detox

Science confirms: Fasting 16+ hours triggers "autophagy" – body eats old dead cells, repairs itself. Reduces diabetes risk, blood pressure, inflammation. Stomach gets rest, liver detoxes.

Prophet's Sunnah: He said "Fast, you will be healthy." 1400 years ago. Now Harvard, Johns Hopkins research same thing. Intermittent fasting = Islamic fasting. Deen was always ahead of science.

8

Benefit 8: Feel Poverty - Develop Empathy

Rich person never knows hunger of poor. Fasting 1 day = you feel what 800 million people feel daily. This creates empathy, increases Sadaqah, reduces arrogance. Heart softens.

Social benefit: When you are hungry at 5PM, you understand beggar at door. You give more. Ummah becomes one body. Fasting kills class difference for 30 days.

9

Benefit 9: Train Nafs - Discipline School

If you can control hunger 16 hours, you can control tongue, eyes, anger whole year. Ramadan is boot camp. 30 days training = 11 months discipline. That's why Sahaba prepared 6 months before Ramadan.

After Ramadan: Keep 2 Sunnah fasts weekly – Monday + Thursday. Prophet fasted them because deeds presented to Allah those days. Keep habit alive.

10

Benefit 10: Special Sunnah Fasts - Extra Rewards

6 days Shawwal: Fast 6 days after Eid = reward of full year fasting. 10th Muharram Ashura: Fast 9+10 or 10+11 = sins of 1 year forgiven. 9th Dhul Hijjah Arafah: For non-Hajjis = sins of 2 years forgiven. 13-14-15 monthly: "White days" = reward of full month fasting.

Year plan: Ramadan 30 + Shawwal 6 + Ashura 1 + Arafah 1 + 12 months x 3 white days = 76 days fasting = reward of 760 days = 2+ years worship in just 76 days.

5 Tips for Easy Fasting

1. Suhoor: Even 3 dates + water. Prophet said "Suhoor has barakah". 2. Hydrate: Drink 2L water between Iftar-Suhoor. 3. Avoid heavy Iftar: Start with dates, light food. Heavy meal = lazy Tarawih. 4. Sleep early: Wake fresh for Fajr + Suhoor. 5. Keep busy: Read Quran, Dhikr. Idle mind thinks about food.

Conclusion: Fall in Love with Fasting

Don't see fasting as punishment. See it as VIP pass to Allah. 16 hours hunger = eternal Jannah. Temporary thirst = river Kawthar. Empty stomach = full reward.

Next Monday is coming. Fast it. Feel difference. Then Thursday. Then 13th of month. After 3 months, fasting becomes addiction – sweet addiction of Iman.

"Fasting is for Allah" – Start Sunnah fast next Monday!
