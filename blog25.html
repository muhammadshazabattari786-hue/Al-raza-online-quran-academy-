Power of Dhikr - Remember Allah
SubhanAllah Alhamdulillah - Easiest Ibadah with Maximum Reward
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 15 Min Read 📚 Dhikrullah ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Dhikr - The Heart's Oxygen

Allah says: "O you who have believed, remember Allah with much remembrance." Surah Ahzab 41. Prophet (PBUH) said: "Should I tell you best deed, purest to your King, highest in ranks, better than spending gold/silver, better than fighting Jihad?" Sahaba said Yes. He said: "Remembrance of Allah." Tirmidhi.

Dhikr means remembering Allah. Tongue says SubhanAllah, heart feels Allah is watching. No wudu needed, no direction needed, no time needed. Walking, driving, cooking, waiting – 24/7 ibadah possible. Yet we waste time on phone while Jannah trees await planting.

This article covers 8 powerful Azkar with exact reward. 2 minutes daily = palace in Jannah. Let's start planting trees.

فَاذْكُرُونِي أَذْكُرْكُمْ وَاشْكُرُوا لِي وَلَا تَكْفُرُونِ
"So remember Me; I will remember you. And be grateful to Me and do not deny Me."
Surah Al-Baqarah, Ayah 152

Allah makes deal: You remember Me, I remember you. When King of Kings remembers YOU among billions, what more honor do you need? Let's learn 8 Dhikr.

1
سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ
SubhanAllah
Glory be to Allah
🌴 Reward: Every SubhanAllah = 1 tree planted in Jannah. Hadith Tirmidhi. 100x daily = 100 trees. On Qiyamah you will have forest.
2
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
Alhamdulillah
All praise is for Allah
🌴 Reward: Fills the scales on Day of Judgment. Also: "Alhamdulillah fills what is between heaven and earth." Muslim. Weight of whole universe in your scale.
3
اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ
Allahu Akbar
Allah is Greatest
🌴 Reward: Heaviest on scales. When you say Allahu Akbar, Shaytan runs away farting. Protects from arrogance – reminds you Allah is bigger than your problem.
4
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ
La ilaha illAllah
There is no god but Allah
🌴 Reward: Best Dhikr. If 7 heavens + 7 earths put on one side of scale, La ilaha illAllah on other side, Kalima outweighs all. Hadith. Also key to Jannah.
5
سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ
SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi
Glory be to Allah and praise Him
🌴 Reward: 100x morning/evening = sins forgiven even if like foam of sea. Also: 2 words light on tongue, heavy on scales, beloved to Rahman. Bukhari.
6
سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ الْعَظِيمِ
SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi, SubhanAllahil-Azeem
Glory be to Allah and praise Him, Glory be to Allah the Magnificent
🌴 Reward: Prophet said these 2 phrases are beloved to Allah. Say after every prayer 3x. Scales will be heavy on Qiyamah.
7
لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ
La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah
There is no power nor strength except with Allah
🌴 Reward: Treasure from treasures of Paradise. Also: Cure for 99 diseases, least is anxiety. Hadith. When stressed, repeat 100x – peace descends.
8
أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ
Astaghfirullah
I seek forgiveness from Allah
🌴 Reward: Whoever makes Istighfar constant, Allah gives relief from distress, way out of difficulty, and sustenance from where he never imagined. Abu Dawud.

The Ultimate Dhikr: 100x Daily Combo

Prophet taught Fatima (RA): After every prayer, 33x SubhanAllah, 33x Alhamdulillah, 34x Allahu Akbar = 100 total. Takes 2 minutes. Reward: Sins forgiven, strength for whole day, no fatigue.

Morning + Evening bonus: 100x SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi. Total 200 daily = 200 trees in Jannah daily = 73,000 trees yearly. In 10 years = 730,000 trees. Your Jannah garden ready!

When to Make Dhikr - 24/7 Opportunities

1. After Salah: 100 combo. 2. Morning/Evening: Azkar time. 3. Driving: Red light = SubhanAllah 10x. 4. Cooking: Stir pot = Alhamdulillah. 5. Before sleep: 33-33-34 on fingers. 6. Anxiety: La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah 100x. 7. After sin: Astaghfirullah immediately.

7 Benefits of Constant Dhikr

1. Heart peace: "Verily in remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest." Ra'd 28. 2. Shaytan runs: Dhikr is like fire for Shaytan. 3. Allah remembers you: Hadith Qudsi. 4. Protection: Dhikr is fortress. 5. Forgiveness: Wipes sins like water. 6. Love of Allah: He loves those who remember Him. 7. Light on face: Muhajir said "Face of Dhakir glows".

3 Mistakes in Dhikr

1. Counting on fingers wrong: Prophet counted on right hand fingers – each joint = reward witness on Qiyamah. 2. Rushing without heart: 10 slow Dhikr with Khushu > 1000 fast without heart. 3. Only after Salah: Dhikr is 24/7, not 5 times only.

21-Day Dhikr Challenge

Day 1-7: Only SubhanAllah 100x after Fajr. Day 8-14: Add Alhamdulillah 100x after Maghrib. Day 15-21: Add 33-33-34 after every Salah. After 21 days, tongue will automatically do Dhikr like breathing. Try it.

Conclusion: Make Tongue Wet with Dhikr

Companions asked Prophet: "Which Muslims are best?" He said: "Those whose tongues are moist with Dhikr of Allah." Tirmidhi. Check your tongue now – moist with Dhikr or gossip/phone?

Start now. Say SubhanAllah 10x. Feel peace? That's Allah remembering you back. Continue whole life and Jannah garden will be waiting.

"Dhikr is life of heart" – Start 100x daily combo today!
