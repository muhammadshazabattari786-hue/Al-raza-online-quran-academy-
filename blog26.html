Zakat - Purification of Wealth
2.5% Only - Purifies 100% Wealth & Removes Barakah Blockage
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 18 Min Read 📚 Zakat ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Zakat - 3rd Pillar, 2.5% Only

Allah ordered: "And establish prayer and give Zakat." Quran mentions Salah + Zakat together 27 times. Why? Because body worship = Salah, wealth worship = Zakat. If you pray 5 times but don't give Zakat, Islam is incomplete.

Zakat = 2.5% of wealth saved 1 year above Nisab. Have $10,000 for 1 year? Give $250 only. Keep $9,750. Allah doesn't ask 20%, 50%. Just 2.5% to purify 100%. Yet many rich Muslims skip it thinking "wealth will decrease". Reality: Zakat increases barakah, no Zakat = wealth gets cursed.

This article explains Zakat rules simply: Who must pay, how to calculate, where to give, 7 benefits, and warning for those who skip. Read carefully – your Akhirah depends on it.

خُذْ مِنْ أَمْوَالِهِمْ صَدَقَةً تُطَهِّرُهُمْ وَتُزَكِّيهِم بِهَا
"Take from their wealth a charity to purify them and cause them increase."
Surah At-Tawbah, Ayah 103

Allah says Zakat "purifies and increases". Purifies your wealth from haram mix, purifies your heart from greed, increases barakah. Let's understand 8 key points.

1

Point 1: Who Must Pay Zakat - Nisab Rule

Nisab = 85 grams gold ≈ $7,000-$8,000 today. If you have cash, gold, silver, business stock, crypto worth Nisab for 1 full lunar year, Zakat is obligatory 2.5%.

Example: $10,000 saved since Ramadan last year. Ramadan comes again = 1 year complete = pay $250 Zakat. Salary used monthly? No Zakat. Only SAVED wealth for 1 year.

2

Point 2: How to Calculate - Simple 2.5% Formula

Formula: Total Zakatable wealth x 0.025 = Zakat. Zakatable = Cash + Bank + Gold/Silver + Business inventory + Crypto + Stocks - Debts due.

Easy method: $40,000 total x 2.5% = $1,000 Zakat. $100,000 x 2.5% = $2,500. Just divide by 40. Our academy gives free Zakat calculator – fill amount, get result instantly.

3

Point 3: 8 Categories Who Can Receive Zakat

Quran 9:60: 1. Fuqara – poor, 2. Masakeen – needy, 3. Zakat collectors, 4. New Muslims, 5. To free slaves, 6. Debtors, 7. In path of Allah, 8. Travelers stranded.

Priority: Poor relatives first, then local poor, then organizations. Cannot give to parents, children, husband/wife. Can give to siblings, cousins, friends if poor.

4

Point 4: When to Pay - Hawl Anniversary

Zakat due after 1 lunar year passes on Nisab amount. Many pay in Ramadan for barakah, but if your "Zakat year" ends in Muharram, pay in Muharram. Delay without reason = sin.

Tip: Fix date – 1st Ramadan or your birthday. Calculate yearly same date. Easier to remember. Set phone reminder 1 week before.

5

Point 5: What Wealth is Zakatable vs Exempt

Zakatable: Cash, bank balance, gold jewelry, silver, business stock, investment, crypto, rental income saved. Not Zakatable: House you live in, car you drive, furniture, clothes, salary spent monthly.

Jewelry: If gold worn regularly, Hanafi says Zakat due. If kept as investment, Zakat definitely due. 85g gold = ~21 tola. Less than that, no Zakat on jewelry.

6

Point 6: 7 Benefits of Paying Zakat

1. Purifies wealth: Removes haram 2-3% mixed unknowingly. 2. Purifies heart: Removes greed, love of money. 3. Increases barakah: $9,750 with Zakat > $10,000 without. 4. Helps Ummah: Poverty reduction system. 5. Protection: Hadith: "Wealth is protected by Zakat". 6. Social justice: Rich help poor. 7. Reward: Sadaqah reward + obligatory reward.

7

Point 7: Warning - Punishment for Not Paying

⚠️ Severe Warning from Quran & Hadith

Allah says: "Those who hoard gold and silver and don't spend in way of Allah – give them news of painful punishment. Day when metal heated in Hell fire, their foreheads, sides, backs branded with it." Tawbah 34-35.

Hadith: "Whoever has wealth and doesn't pay Zakat, on Qiyamah wealth will become snake biting his neck." Bukhari. Zakat is not charity – it's right of poor in your wealth. Withholding = stealing from poor.

8

Point 8: Zakat vs Sadaqah - Difference

Zakat: 2.5%, obligatory, only for 8 categories, yearly. Sadaqah: Any amount, voluntary, anyone, anytime. Zakat is Fardh like Salah. Sadaqah is Nafl like Nafil prayer. Both needed.

Mindset: Don't think "I give Sadaqah so Zakat not needed". Zakat is debt to Allah. Sadaqah is bonus. Pay Zakat first, then Sadaqah as much as you can.

Common Zakat Questions Answered

Q1: Zakat on provident fund/pension? A: If you can access it, pay Zakat yearly on full amount. Q2: Zakat on house for rent? A: Pay 2.5% on yearly rent collected + value of house if for sale. Q3: Can I give Zakat to mosque? A: Yes if mosque helps poor, orphans. Building only = scholars differ, better give to poor.

Example Calculation 2026

Ahmed has: Cash $5,000 + Bank $8,000 + Gold 100g worth $8,500 + Business stock $15,000 = Total $36,500. Minus debt $1,500 = $35,000. Zakat = $35,000 ÷ 40 = $875. Pay before Ramadan ends. Simple.

وَمَا أَنفَقْتُم مِّن شَيْءٍ فَهُوَ يُخْلِفُهُ وَهُوَ خَيْرُ الرَّازِقِينَ
"And whatever you spend, He will replace it; and He is the best of providers."
Surah Saba, Ayah 39

Conclusion: 2.5% is Not Loss, It's Investment

We spend 30% on tax, 20% on shopping, 10% on phone – no problem. But 2.5% for Allah feels "heavy". Change mindset: Zakat is not expense, it's investment with Allah who never goes bankrupt.

Calculate your Zakat today. If due, pay immediately. If not due yet, set reminder. And make dua: "Allahumma barik li fi rizqi" – O Allah bless my wealth. Wealth with Zakat = Halal + Barakah + Jannah. Without Zakat = Curse + Anxiety + Punishment.

"Zakat purifies wealth" – Calculate & pay your Zakat today!
