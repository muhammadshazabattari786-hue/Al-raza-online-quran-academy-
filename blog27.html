Hajj & Umrah - Journey to Allah
5th Pillar Complete Guide - Once in Lifetime Obligation
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 20 Min Read 📚 Hajj Umrah ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Hajj - Pillar of Once Lifetime

Allah says: "Pilgrimage to the House is duty upon mankind for the sake of Allah, upon whoever is able to find a way." Surah Aal-Imran 97. Prophet (PBUH) said: "Islam is built on 5 pillars... and Hajj to House for who can afford journey." Bukhari.

Hajj is 5th pillar but only once in lifetime if you have physical + financial ability. Umrah is Sunnah, can do anytime. 2 million Muslims gather yearly in Arafat wearing same white Ihram – no king, no poor, all equal before Allah. Most spiritual journey of life.

This article explains Hajj vs Umrah, conditions, 5 main steps of Hajj, 10 benefits, and how to prepare. Even if you cannot go now, read to increase longing and make dua.

وَأَتِمُّوا الْحَجَّ وَالْعُمْرَةَ لِلَّهِ
"And complete Hajj and Umrah for Allah."
Surah Al-Baqarah, Ayah 196

Allah commands to "complete" – don't start and leave. Hajj has rules. Let's understand basics first.

1

Point 1: Hajj vs Umrah - Key Differences

Hajj: 5-6 days in Dhul Hijjah 8-13. Obligatory once. Includes Arafat, Muzdalifah, Mina, Rami, Qurbani. Umrah: 2-3 hours anytime. Sunnah. Only Ihram, Tawaf 7 rounds, Sa'i 7 rounds, shave/cut hair. No Arafat, no Mina.

Reward: Hajj Mabroor = no reward except Jannah. Umrah to Umrah = expiation for sins between them. Both wipe sins like newborn.

2

Point 2: Who Must Do Hajj - 5 Conditions

1. Muslim 2. Baligh adult 3. Sane 4. Free not slave 5. Istita'ah – physical + financial ability. If $8,000-$10,000 for package + family expenses while away, Hajj obligatory.

Women: Need Mahram husband/father/brother/son. Without Mahram, Hajj not obligatory. Can do Umrah with Mahram anytime.

3

Point 3: 5 Main Steps of Hajj - Simplified

Day 8: Enter Ihram, go Mina, stay night. Day 9 Arafat: Most important day. Stand, cry, make dua from Zuhr to Maghrib. Allah forgives millions. Night 9: Muzdalifah, collect pebbles. Day 10 Eid: Rami Jamarat, Qurbani, shave, Tawaf Ifadah. Day 11-13: Stay Mina, Rami 3 days.

Secret: Arafat is Hajj. Prophet said "Al-Hajju Arafah". If you miss Arafat, Hajj invalid. All other steps can be made up.

4

Point 4: Ihram - Dress of Equality

Men: 2 white unstitched cloths. Women: Simple dress covering all except face+hands. No perfume, no cutting hair/nails, no marital relations. Why? To remember death – we all buried in white cloth. To kill ego – no designer clothes, no status.

Feeling: When 2 million wear same dress, color/race/rich/poor differences vanish. Only "Labbaik Allahumma Labbaik" – Here I am O Allah.

5

Point 5: Tawaf - Circling House of Allah

7 rounds anti-clockwise around Kaaba. Start at Black Stone, say Bismillah Allahu Akbar. First 3 rounds men walk fast, last 4 normal. Why circling? Like angels circling Arsh, like electrons circling nucleus – everything in universe circles Allah.

Dua: No fixed dua. Talk to Allah in your language. "Rabbi, forgive me, guide me, give me Jannah". Each step = sins forgiven + rank raised.

6

Point 6: Sa'i - Running Between Safa-Marwah

7 rounds between two hills. Remembers Hajar (AS) running for water for baby Ismail. Allah made Zamzam spring from Ismail's feet. Lesson: When you run for Allah, Allah creates rivers for you.

Dua at Safa/Marwah: "La ilaha illAllahu wahdahu la shareeka lah..." 3 times. Make dua facing Kaaba. Struggle of mother rewarded eternally.

7

Point 7: Arafat - Day of Forgiveness

Hadith: Day of Arafat
Prophet (PBUH) said: "There is no day on which Allah frees more people from Hell than Day of Arafat." Muslim. Allah boasts to angels about pilgrims.

Strategy: From Zuhr to Maghrib, don't talk, don't eat much. Just dua. Dua list ready: Parents, kids, hidayah, Jannah, forgiveness. Cry even if no tears. Allah sees heart.

8

Point 8: Hajj Mabroor - Accepted Hajj Reward

Hadith: "Hajj Mabroor has no reward except Paradise." Bukhari, Muslim. Sign of accepted Hajj: After returning, person becomes more pious, more humble, leaves sins. If same after Hajj, check intention.

3 things make Hajj Mabroor: 1. Halal money spent 2. No arguing/fighting 3. Perfect obedience to rules. Avoid selfie, shopping, fighting for place in Tawaf.

Quick Umrah Guide - 4 Steps Only

1. Ihram: From Jeddah airport or home. Intention "Labbaik Umrah". 2. Tawaf: 7 rounds Kaaba. 3. Sa'i: 7 rounds Safa-Marwah. 4. Halq/Taqseer: Men shave/cut hair, women cut 1 inch. Umrah complete. Total 2-3 hours.

Best time: Ramadan Umrah = reward of Hajj with Prophet. But crowded + expensive. Off-season Umrah = peaceful, cheaper.

10 Benefits of Hajj & Umrah

1. Sins wiped: Like newborn baby. 2. Dua accepted: Pilgrim's dua not rejected. 3. Guests of Allah: Hadith: "Pilgrims are guests of Allah". 4. Reward x100,000: 1 prayer in Haram = 100,000 prayers. 5. Unity: Ummah comes together. 6. Patience training: Crowd teaches Sabr. 7. Gratitude: See simplicity, value blessings. 8. Death reminder: Ihram = kafan. 9. Barakah in wealth: Spending in Allah's path returns multiplied. 10. Protection: Dies in Haram = dies Shaheed.

5 Mistakes to Avoid

1. Wrong intention: "For status/photo" ruins reward. 2. Arguing: Prophet said avoid Rafath, Fusooq, Jidal. 3. Missing Arafat: Most critical. 4. Rushing rituals: Take time, feel spirituality. 5. Backbiting after return: "Haji became rude". Don't waste reward.

How to Prepare If You Can't Go Yet

1. Save money: $100 monthly = $6,000 in 5 years. 2. Learn Manasik: Watch videos, read book. 3. Make dua: "Allahumma arzuqni Hajj" daily. 4. Reduce sins: Hajj requires halal money + pure heart. 5. Send others: Sponsor poor person Hajj = you get same reward.

Conclusion: Make Intention Today

If Allah called you to His House, you are chosen among billions. Start saving, start learning. Even if you die before going but had sincere intention, Allah will give reward of Hajj. Hadith: "Actions are by intentions".

Say now: "Ya Allah, write Hajj for me before death." That dua itself is worship. One day you will stand in Arafat crying, and say "Alhamdulillah I made intention years ago."

"Labbaik Allahumma Labbaik" – Make intention for Hajj today!
