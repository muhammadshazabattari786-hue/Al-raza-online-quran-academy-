Tawakkul - Trust in Allah
Tie Your Camel + Trust Allah - Balance of Effort & Faith
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 16 Min Read 📚 Tawakkul ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Tawakkul - Not Laziness, Not Stress

A man asked Prophet (PBUH): "Should I tie my camel and trust Allah, or leave it untied and trust Allah?" Prophet said: "Tie it, then trust Allah." Tirmidhi. This one hadith destroys 2 extremes: Laziness "Allah will do" and Anxiety "I must control everything".

Tawakkul = Do your 100% effort, then leave result to Allah with full trust. Like farmer: plow, seed, water – that's effort. Rain, growth – that's Allah. You control effort, Allah controls result. When we confuse these, we get depression or disappointment.

This article teaches 8 principles of true Tawakkul with Quran + examples of Prophets. Learn to sleep peacefully even in storms.

وَمَن يَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ فَهُوَ حَسْبُهُ
"And whoever relies upon Allah - then He is sufficient for him."
Surah At-Talaq, Ayah 3

Allah promises: If you trust Him truly, He becomes sufficient for you. Job, money, health, problem – Allah handles. Let's learn how.

1

Point 1: Tawakkul Definition - 2 Parts

Part 1 - Asbab: Take means. Sick? Go doctor + medicine. Jobless? Apply + interview. Exam? Study hard. This is "tying camel". Part 2 - Tawakkul: After effort, say "Ya Allah result is in Your hands". Don't stress "What if I fail?".

Wrong Tawakkul: Not studying and saying "Allah will pass me". That's laziness, not Tawakkul. Right Tawakkul: Study 10 hours + sleep peacefully trusting Allah.

2

Point 2: Example of Prophet Ibrahim (AS) - Fire Tawakkul

When thrown in fire, Jibreel asked "Need help?" Ibrahim said "Hasbiyallahu wa ni'mal wakeel" – Allah is sufficient for me. He didn't jump himself, but when thrown, he trusted Allah. Result: Fire became cool and safe.

Lesson: You do your part – don't jump in fire. But when problem hits despite effort, say Hasbiyallah. Allah can turn fire to garden.

3

Point 3: Example of Prophet Musa (AS) - Sea Tawakkul

Pharaoh army behind, Red Sea front. People panicked "We are doomed!" Musa said "Never! My Lord is with me, He will guide me." Allah split sea. Musa didn't have boat, didn't swim – just trusted.

Your "sea": Debt, illness, unemployment. Take means you can, then say Musa's words. Allah can split any "sea" for you.

4

Point 4: 7 Signs You Have True Tawakkul

1. Work hard but sleep peacefully: No 3AM anxiety. 2. Happy with result: If get job = Alhamdulillah. If not = Alhamdulillah, Allah has better. 3. Don't beg people: Ask Allah first. 4. No jealousy: Others' success doesn't hurt you. 5. Calm in crisis: Storm comes, heart stable. 6. Gratitude in loss: "Allah took, Allah can give". 7. Optimistic: Always hope in Allah's mercy.

5

Point 5: Bird Example - Ultimate Tawakkul Teacher

Hadith: Bird Tawakkul
Prophet (PBUH) said: "If you trusted Allah as you should, He would provide for you like birds. They leave hungry morning, return full evening." Tirmidhi

Bird doesn't have bank account, CV, job. But flies out daily seeking rizq. Allah feeds it. You have brain, skills, degree – trust Allah and work. Rizq is written, just chase it with effort.

6

Point 6: Tawakkul vs Laziness - Clear Difference

Laziness: "No job? Allah will provide" while sleeping all day.
Tawakkul: "No job? Apply 20 places daily + pray Istikhara + trust Allah's timing."

Laziness: "No cure? Allah heals" without doctor.
Tawakkul: "Allah heals, so take medicine + dua + trust."

Umar (RA) saw people in Masjid doing no work. He beat them saying "Sky doesn't rain gold and silver. Work!" Sahaba had best Tawakkul AND best work ethic.

7

Point 7: Dua of Tawakkul - Say This Daily

Prophet taught: "Allahumma inni aslamtu nafsi ilaik, wa fawwadtu amri ilaik" – O Allah I submit myself to You and entrust my affairs to You. Say before sleep. Hand over tomorrow's stress to Allah tonight.

Hasbiyallah: "Allah is sufficient for me" – 7x morning/evening. This one line removes 100 worries. Try for 7 days.

8

Point 8: When Result is "No" - Wisdom of Allah

You applied job with Tawakkul, didn't get. 2 options: 1. Allah saving you from haram environment. 2. Better job coming later. 3. Test of patience for higher rank in Jannah.

Story: Man missed flight, was angry. Plane crashed. He cried "Alhamdulillah for Tawakkul". What you call "loss" might be Allah's protection. Trust His choice more than yours.

5 Steps to Build Tawakkul Today

1. Identify worry: Write main stress on paper. 2. Take action: What 1 step you can take today? Do it. 3. Say dua: "Fawwadtu amri ilaik" – I entrust matter to You. 4. Stop overthinking: After effort, thinking again = no Tawakkul. 5. Say Alhamdulillah: On result whatever it is.

3 Mistakes Killing Tawakkul

1. Checking phone 50x: Applied job, then check email every 5 min. That's no trust. Check 2x daily, rest leave to Allah. 2. Asking people repeatedly: "Any update?" Ask Allah in Tahajjud instead. 3. Saying "I must control": You control effort only. Result is Allah's department.

40-Day Tawakkul Challenge

For 40 days: Morning say "Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa Huwa alayhi tawakkaltu" 7x. Night say "Fawwadtu amri ilaik" before sleep. When worry comes, say "Allah is sufficient". After 40 days, anxiety level will drop 80% InshaAllah.

Conclusion: Sleep Like Baby, Work Like Lion

Baby cries, mother feeds – baby doesn't worry "how milk will come". Be baby before Allah. Work hard like lion in day, sleep peacefully like baby at night because "My Lord is watching".

Tie your camel today – send CV, take medicine, study. Then say "Ya Allah I did my part, now Your turn." And watch how Allah handles what you cannot.

"Allah is sufficient for me" – Start Tawakkul today!
