Patience - Sabr in Islam
Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un - Key to Jannah
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 17 Min Read 📚 Sabr ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Sabr - Half of Iman

Allah mentions "Sabr" 90+ times in Quran. "O you who have believed, seek help through patience and prayer." Baqarah 153. Prophet (PBUH) said: "Patience is light" and "No gift better than patience." Bukhari, Muslim.

Sabr = Restraining tongue from complaining, heart from anger, limbs from haram. 3 types: Patience on obedience, patience from sin, patience on calamity. Without Sabr, Salah feels heavy, sins feel sweet, trials break you.

This article covers 3 types of Sabr, unlimited reward, Prophet Ayyub example, and 7 ways to build patience. If you master Sabr, you master life.

إِنَّمَا يُوَفَّى الصَّابِرُونَ أَجْرَهُم بِغَيْرِ حِسَابٍ
"Indeed, the patient will be given their reward without account."
Surah Az-Zumar, Ayah 10

Other deeds have limit: Salah 10x, Sadaqah 700x. But Sabr? Allah says "without account" – unlimited, no calculator. That's why Sabr is most beloved deed to Allah.

1

Type 1: Sabr on Obedience - Waking for Fajr

Waking at 5AM for Fajr when bed is warm = Sabr. Fasting 16 hours = Sabr. Praying 20 rakah Tarawih when legs hurt = Sabr. Controlling eyes when phone shows haram = Sabr.

Reward: Every second of obedience Sabr = Jannah rank. Hadith: "Wudu in hardship, walking to Masjid, waiting prayer after prayer – that is Ribat, that is Ribat." Muslim.

2

Type 2: Sabr from Sin - Saying "No" to Haram

Girl messages you late night – you block = Sabr. Job offers bribe – you refuse = Sabr. Friend offers cigarette – you walk away = Sabr. Eyes see haram on street – you lower gaze = Sabr.

Hadith: 7 People Under Allah's Shade
One is "man invited by beautiful woman but says I fear Allah". That's Sabr from sin = Arsh shade on Qiyamah.
3

Type 3: Sabr on Calamity - Inna lillahi...

Death, illness, job loss, divorce, exam fail – first moment reaction is real Sabr. Prophet said: "True Sabr is at first strike of calamity." Bukhari. After 1 week crying is normal, but first second matters.

Magic words: "Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un" – We belong to Allah and to Him we return. Say this + "Allahumma ajirni fi musibati" – Allah gives better replacement.

Story: Prophet Ayyub (AS) - 18 Years Sabr

Allah tested Ayyub: Lost all wealth, all children died, body covered in painful disease for 18 years. People left him. Wife stayed, served him. One day she said "Ask Allah to cure". Ayyub said: "Allah gave me 70 years health, I am shy to ask for cure before equal time of patience."

Result: Allah cured him, returned wealth double, gave children double. Quran calls him "patient, excellent servant". Sad 44. Your 2-year problem is nothing vs 18 years. Learn Sabr from him.

7 Ways to Build Patience Today

1. Remember reward: "Without account" – visualize unlimited Hasanat bank. 2. Make dua: "Allahumma alhimni sabran" – O Allah inspire me patience. 3. Compare: Your problem vs Ayyub, vs Gaza children, vs cancer patient. Be grateful. 4. Say Alhamdulillah: On good and bad. 5. Read Quran: Especially Surah Yusuf – full story of Sabr. 6. Keep company: Sit with patient people, not complainers. 7. Know this is test: Allah tests those He loves. Hadith Tirmidhi.

5 Rewards of Sabr No One Tells You

1. Allah is with you: "Indeed Allah is with the patient." Baqarah 153. 2. Sins wiped: Every pain, thorn, sadness = expiation. 3. Rank raised: Patience = shortcut to high Jannah. 4. Victory: "Victory comes with patience." Hadith. 5. Love of Allah: "Allah loves the patient." Aal-Imran 146.

What Breaks Sabr - 4 Things to Avoid

1. Complaining to people: "My life is ruined" on Facebook = Sabr lost. Complain to Allah only – like Yaqub (AS) "I complain of my grief to Allah only". 2. Slapping face/tearing clothes: Prophet said not from us. 3. Impatience in dua: "I made dua 100x, nothing". Give up = no Sabr. 4. Despair: "Allah forgot me". No – delay is Allah's wisdom.

Dua of Patience from Quran

رَبَّنَا أَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا
"Our Lord, pour upon us patience and plant firmly our feet."
Surah Al-Baqarah, Ayah 250

Taleut's army made this dua before battle. Say it in Sajdah when problem hits. Allah will pour patience like rain.

30-Day Sabr Challenge

Day 1-10: When angry, stay silent 10 seconds + Astaghfirullah. Day 11-20: When problem hits, first words = Inna lillahi. Day 21-30: Thank Allah for 1 "bad" thing daily. After 30 days, Sabr becomes habit.

Conclusion: Sabr is Not Weakness, It's Power

World says "Be strong, fight back". Islam says "Be patient, Allah fights for you". Sabr doesn't mean don't cry – Prophet cried at son's death. Sabr means don't say what angers Allah.

Next time trial hits, smile and say "Ya Allah, give me Sabr + reward without account". That one sentence can take you to Firdaws. Patience is bitter now, but its fruit is sweeter than honey in Jannah.

"Indeed Allah is with the patient" – Practice Sabr today!
