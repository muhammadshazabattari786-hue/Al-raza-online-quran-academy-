Death & Akhirah - Life After Death
Grave to Jannah Journey - Prepare Before Last Breath
📅 April 2026 ⏱️ 22 Min Read 📚 Akhirah ✍️ Al Raza Academy

Introduction: Death - The Reality Everyone Avoids

Allah says: "Every soul will taste death." Aal-Imran 185. Prophet (PBUH) said: "Remember often the destroyer of pleasures – death." Tirmidhi. We plan for retirement at 60, but death can come at 20, 30, today.

This is final blog of 30. We learned Salah, Fasting, Zakat, Hajj, Dhikr, Sabr, Tawakkul. But all is useless if we don't prepare for Akhirah. Life is exam hall, death is paper submission, grave is result waiting room, Qiyamah is result day.

This article takes you on journey: Last breath → Grave → Qiyamah → Jannah/Jahannam. Read with open heart. Maybe this blog changes your life before Angel of Death comes.

كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ ثُمَّ إِلَيْنَا تُرْجَعُونَ
"Every soul will taste death. Then to Us you will be returned."
Surah Al-Ankabut, Ayah 57

Death is not end – it's transfer from Dunya class to Akhirah class. Let's see 10 stages after death.

1

Stage 1: Sakaratul Maut - Pangs of Death

Quran: "And the intoxication of death comes in truth." Qaf 19. Even Prophet (PBUH) felt pain, said "La ilaha illAllah, indeed death has pangs". Bukhari. For believer = sins wiped. For sinner = punishment starts.

Signs: Angel of Death comes. Believer sees angels of mercy with white face. Sinner sees angels of punishment with black face. Last words decide fate – that's why say La ilaha illAllah daily so it comes naturally.

2

Stage 2: Barzakh - Life in Grave

Between death and Qiyamah = Barzakh. Grave is either garden of Jannah or pit of Hell. Hadith: "Grave is first stage of Akhirah. If easy, next stages easy. If hard, next harder." Tirmidhi.

Believer's grave: Expanded 70x70 arms, filled with light, door to Jannah opens, fragrance comes. Sleeps like bridegroom waiting wedding. Sinner's grave: Squeezed until ribs crack, snakes, scorpions, fire. Asks "When is Qiyamah?" hoping punishment ends.

3

Stage 3: Munkar Nakir - 3 Questions

Two angels with blue-black faces come. Ask: 1. Man Rabbuk? Who is your Lord? 2. Ma dinuk? What is your religion? 3. Ma hadha ar-rajul? Who is this man sent to you? Prophet Muhammad.

Answer: Only those who lived Islam can say "My Lord Allah, my Deen Islam, my Prophet Muhammad". Others stutter "I heard people saying...". Then hammer hits 70x. May Allah make our tongue firm.

4

Stage 4: Soor - Trumpet Blow

Angel Israfil has trumpet at mouth since creation, waiting command. First blow = everyone dies except Allah wills. Second blow = everyone resurrected. 40 years between blows – only Allah knows.

Scene: Graves split, people rise dust on head like drunk. First to rise = Prophet Muhammad. We will follow him InshaAllah if we followed Sunnah.

5

Stage 5: Hashr - Gathering

All humans from Adam to last person gathered barefoot, naked, uncircumcised on plain land. Sun brought close 1 mile. People drown in sweat according to deeds. Some ankle deep, some neck deep.

Shade: Only 7 types get shade of Arsh: Just ruler, youth worshipped Allah, heart attached to Masjid, etc. Others suffer. That's why do good now.

6

Stage 6: Hisab - Accountability

Every deed weighed on scales. Smallest atom good/bad seen. Book of deeds given in right hand = success. Left hand/behind back = failure. Allah shows video of your life – you cannot deny.

⚠️ Danger: Debts Not Forgiven

Shahid dies martyr, all sins forgiven except debt. Hadith Muslim. Borrow money and die without paying = stuck. Pay debts now. Also rights of people – backbiting, cheating – need forgiveness from person, not just Allah.

7

Stage 7: Siraat - Bridge Over Hell

Bridge thinner than hair, sharper than sword over Hell. Everyone must cross. Believers with light run like lightning, wind, horse. Hypocrites fall into Hell. Mother, father, friend – no one helps. "Nafsi Nafsi" – myself.

Speed depends on: Salah, Zakat, good character. Those who helped people in Dunya, Allah helps them cross fast.

8

Stage 8: Hawd - Pond of Kauthar

Prophet's pond, whiter than milk, sweeter than honey. Whoever drinks once never thirsty again. Believers drink, Prophet recognizes by wudu marks glowing on face/hands/feet.

Innovators: Those who changed Deen after Prophet will be driven away. Prophet will say "My Ummah!" Angels reply "You don't know what they innovated after you."

Stage 9-10: Jannah vs Jahannam - Final Destination

Jahannam: 7 gates, each for different sinners. Food = Zaqqum tree, drink = boiling water + pus. Skin burns, replaced new skin to feel pain again. Worst punishment = separation from Allah's vision.

Jannah: 8 gates, 100 levels. Rivers of milk, honey, wine, water. Fruits as you wish. "What no eye seen, no ear heard, no heart imagined." Bukhari. Highest = Firdaws, under Arsh. Greatest blessing = seeing Allah's face.

7 Things to Prepare Before Death

1. Tawbah now: Don't delay. Death no appointment. 2. Pay debts: Financial + rights of people. 3. Write will: Islamic inheritance. 4. Learn Kalima: Last words must be La ilaha illAllah. 5. Sadaqah Jariyah: Build well, teach Quran – reward after death. 6. Righteous children: They will make dua for you. 7. Love for Allah: Die with heart attached to Allah, not Dunya.

Final Advice: Live Like Traveler

Prophet advised Ibn Umar: "Be in this world like stranger or traveler." Bukhari. Traveler doesn't buy furniture for hotel room. We are travelers to Akhirah. Dunya hotel – use it, don't love it.

30 blogs complete. From basics to Akhirah. Now choose: Will you be among those who say "Ya Allah send me back to do good"? Or among those Allah says "Welcome O peaceful soul, enter My Jannah"?

"O reassured soul, return to your Lord, well-pleased and pleasing."
Surah Al-Fajr, Ayah 27-28

"Death is appointment we all must keep" – Prepare today, not tomorrow!
