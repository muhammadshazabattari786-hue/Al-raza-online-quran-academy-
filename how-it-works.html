About Al Raza Online Quran Academy

We are a premium international Quran academy providing one-on-one live classes to students worldwide. Our mission is to make Quran learning easy, accessible and enjoyable for all ages.

With certified teachers, flexible schedules and a friendly learning environment, we ensure every student achieves their Quranic goals with confidence.

Joining Al Raza Online Quran Academy is very easy. No complex procedures. Just follow these steps:

1

📝 Fill Registration Form

Start by filling our simple registration form below. It takes only 2-3 minutes. Provide your basic details, course preference and preferred class timing.

  • Select your country and timezone
2

📧 Receive Confirmation Email

After form submission, our admin team will send you confirmation email within 2 hours. This email contains your trial class schedule and Zoom/WhatsApp joining link.

  • Check inbox and spam/junk folder
  • Email sent from official academy address
  • 24/7 support available for queries
3

🎁 Attend 3 Days Free Trial

Join your 3-day free trial classes with our certified teacher. Test our teaching method, teacher's behavior and class environment completely free.

4

⏰ Select Your Class Schedule

After successful trial, choose your preferred class days and timings according to your timezone and daily routine. We are flexible for all countries.

  • Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night slots

  • Change timing anytime if needed
5

💰 Receive Fee Invoice

We'll send you detailed fee invoice via email with multiple payment options. Fees are very affordable and charged monthly in advance.

  • Bank Transfer, Western Union, PayPal

  • No hidden charges or registration fee

🚀 Start Regular Classes

Last step! After payment confirmation, your classes will start immediately with the assigned teacher. You can start learning Quran from comfort of your home.
